**Project Description:**
Road traffic accidents are a major public safety concern causing loss of life, injuries, and economic damage. This Traffic Crash Analysis Dashboard analyzes multi-year crash data to study trends, severity, weather, geography, and time-based patterns, helping identify high-risk conditions and support data-driven road safety decisions.

**Objectives:**
Analyze traffic crash trends across multiple years
Identify high-risk regions and conditions
Evaluate crash severity and injury patterns
Support informed decision-making for road safety planning

**Dataset**
Source: https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/traffic-crashes-crashes
Records: Multi-year crash data
Key fields: Crash date, location, severity, injuries, weather conditions, vehicle damage,etc.

**Data Cleaning & Transformation**
Removed duplicates and handled missing values
Standardized date and categorical fields
Created calculated columns and DAX measures
Data prepared using Power Query

**Key KPIs**
Total Crashes
Total Injuries
Average Injuries per Crash
Percentage of Severe Crashes

**Visualizations**
KPI Cards for summary metrics
Bar and Line Charts for trend analysis
Monthly crash distribution
Geographic crash distribution
Interactive slicers for filtering

**Key Insights**
Certain regions consistently report higher crash volumes
Severe crashes are more frequent under specific conditions
Monthly trends reveal recurring peak accident periods
